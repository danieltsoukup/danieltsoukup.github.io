academic life



My research at the University of Vienna is centred around understanding large, seemingly random and chaotic abstract mathematical objects by looking at the collection of well-behaving substructures. How do local and global properties of these structures interact and affect each other? I have been focusing on these questions in graph theory, logic and combinatorics in general.
